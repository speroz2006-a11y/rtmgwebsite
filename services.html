We work on monthly agreements with a 30-day notice period. We don't believe in locking clients in — we believe in earning retention through results.

Yes — and we recommend it. The most effective programs use paid search and social together, with content and analytics as the connective tissue. We manage the full picture.

Paid search and social campaigns typically show meaningful data within the first 30–60 days. Content compounds over 3–6 months. We set realistic expectations on day one.
